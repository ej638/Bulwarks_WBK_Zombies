[h1]Initial Publication Changelog[/h1]

[quote]The city is open. The dead have arrived.[/quote]

[h2]Core Release[/h2]
[list]
[*]Released Dynamic Bulwarks rebuilt around WebKnight's Zombies and Creatures
[*]Added a custom WBK adapter, wave factory, scoring bridge, and multiplayer-safe integration layer instead of a simple unit swap
[*]Converted the mission into a zombies-only survival experience centered on scavenging, fortifying, and holding the Bulwark
[*]Included the compatibility pass needed to make WBK synthetic-HP enemies work cleanly inside the Dynamic Bulwarks wave, scoring, revive, and loot systems
[/list]

[h2]New Gameplay and Wave Content[/h2]
[list]
[*]Added tiered infected escalation across shamblers, runners, bloaters, screamers, leapers, smashers, and Goliaths
[*]Added Bloater Rush and Siege Wave special rounds
[*]Added early bloater preview waves so breaching threats appear before the late game
[*]Retuned civilian zombie visuals, spawn distances, movement behavior, and urban pursuit logic for outbreak-style pacing
[/list]

[h2]Fortifications and Breaching[/h2]
[list]
[*]Added barricade synthetic HP so player-built defenses can be worn down and destroyed
[*]Added fortification survival bonuses at wave end for damaged defenses that hold
[*]Fixed build-sell refunds so rebuilding and repair loops are viable
[*]Added contextual bloater barricade targeting and explosion-based breaching
[*]Restored real pressure against overbuilt positions so fortifications buy time instead of permanent safety
[/list]

[h2]Revive, Scoring, and Multiplayer[/h2]
[list]
[*]Bridged WBK melee damage into the Bulwarks revive system so regular zombie hits down players correctly
[*]Stopped zombies from repeatedly dogpiling incapacitated players after a down
[*]Added dedicated server and headless-client safe hit scoring, kill attribution, and last-scorer fallback logic
[*]Added multiplayer-safe hit and kill handling for WBK enemies using synthetic HP and scripted damage
[/list]

[h2]Performance and AI Stability[/h2]
[list]
[*]Added Performance Mode with dynamic zombie caps based on hardware tier and player count
[*]Reduced mission-script scheduler waste, tightened spawn throttling, and improved long-wave server stability
[*]Added WBK-specific stuck detection, idle recovery, targeting refresh, and movement fixes for dense city environments
[*]Improved zombie audio behavior so large groups sound like groups instead of a single misplaced horde source
[/list]

[h2]Loot and Quality of Life[/h2]
[list]
[*]Converted loot generation to curated whitelist pools for cleaner balance and lower mission-start overhead
[*]Increased ammo counts for weapon loot, spin-box rewards, and ammo resupply
[*]Added optional starting weapons on spawn and respawn
[*]Added automatic pickup of compatible magazines when taking weapons from loot containers
[/list]
