[h1]Dynamic Bulwarks: WBK Zombies and Creatures Edition[/h1] 
 
[quote]One run begins in a market square. The next begins in a cramped apartment block with boarded windows and two magazines between the team. The city changes. The pressure does not.[/quote] 
 
[h2]What This Is[/h2] 
Dynamic Bulwarks is a wave-survival mission for Arma 3 built around a simple loop: spawn into a randomly selected city, scavenge what you can, build a defensive position around the Bulwark Box, and survive increasingly dangerous attack waves. 
 
This edition rebuilds that loop around [b]WebKnight's Zombies and Creatures[/b]. It is not a straight classname swap; the wave factory, scoring, revive flow, fortification pressure, multiplayer handling, and performance guardrails were all adapted so WBK's synthetic-HP infected work properly inside the Dynamic Bulwarks framework. 
 
[h2]Map And Porting Note[/h2] 
This release is currently built for [b]Altis[/b]. 
 
The original Dynamic Bulwarks creators also published a guide on manually changing the mission to other maps here:

[h2]Check Parameters Before Starting[/h2]
Before starting a session, hosts should open the mission [b]Parameters[/b] menu and tune the Bulwarks settings to taste. Loot spawn density, downtime between rounds, mission area size, special wave behavior, starting equipment, point values, and other core mission settings are exposed there, so it is worth checking them before your first run.
 
[h2]Core Dynamic Bulwarks Features[/h2] 
[list] 
[*][b]Random city every run[/b] with configurable mission area size and time-of-day settings 
[*][b]Loot, build, defend[/b] gameplay loop with downtime between waves 
[*][b]Scavenge buildings[/b] for weapons, ammunition, gear, explosives, backpacks, and special loot 
[*][b]Point economy[/b] tied to combat performance, spent through the Bulwark Box shop 
[*][b]Base building[/b] with barricades, platforms, towers, bunkers, lights, storage, and static weapons 
[*][b]Support abilities[/b] including Recon UAV, Paratroopers, Missile CAS, Mine Cluster Shell, Rage Stimpack, Emergency Teleport, ARMAKART TM, and Predator Drone 
[*][b]Special loot events[/b] such as the Spin Box, Loot Drone, cash pickups, and the Satellite Dish that unlocks supports 
[*][b]Revive and recovery loop[/b] with bleedout, medikit support, and FAK-to-Medikit conversion at the Bulwark Box 
[*][b]Wave modifiers[/b] and intermission pacing that keep each defense run from feeling identical 
[/list] 
 
[h2]WBK Features Added In This Edition[/h2] 
[list] 
[*][b]Custom WBK wave factory[/b] with budget-based escalation, tiered infected selection, boss cooldowns, and drip-fed overflow spawning 
[*][b]Escalation across multiple infected roles[/b], from shamblers and runners up through bloaters, screamers, leapers, smashers, and Goliaths 
[*][b]Full zombies-only conversion[/b] of the mission flow, removing vanilla vehicle and soldier-wave content that did not fit the outbreak theme 
[*][b]New special waves[/b]: Bloater Rush and Siege Wave 
[*][b]WBK-aware scoring and revive integration[/b] so zombie melee, special attacks, and kills interact correctly with the Bulwarks point and downed-player systems 
[*][b]Zombie targeting, movement, idle recovery, and stuck cleanup tuning[/b] built specifically for dense urban fights 
[*][b]Performance scaling mode[/b] with dynamic zombie caps, spawn throttling, and reduced scheduler waste for better server behavior 
[*][b]Fortification overhaul[/b] with barricade structural HP, sell/refund repair loop, and wave-end survival bonuses for defenses that hold 
[*][b]Contextual bloater breaching[/b]: when a barricade blocks the path, the bloater will try to open it 
[*][b]Curated loot and QoL updates[/b] including configurable weapon pool (all weapons with DLC/mods by default, or vanilla-only for players without DLC), better ammo counts, optional starting weapons, and automatic magazine pickup from loot containers 
[/list] 
 
[h2]WBK Difficulty Tuning[/h2]
WBK zombie HP values, scream cooldowns, headshot multiplier, and other behavior are all adjustable in the mission [b]Parameters[/b] menu before starting. The defaults are tuned for 1-2 player runs, but if the mission feels too punishing for solo play, this is the first place to adjust.
